Delhi Development Authority (DDA) launches new housing schemes with green developments

Delhi Development Authority (DDA) is enhancing green spaces and finalizing unsold flats from recent housing schemes. Over 1,650 flats have been sold in various locations. The authority is also improving infrastructure in Narela and ensuring cleanliness with local bodies. Buyers can book flats through DDA's website or contact their helpline.
NEW DELHI: Receiving an encouraging response for the flats offered in latest three housing schemes launched last month, Delhi Development Authority (DDA) has taken up the task of developing greens around the housing pockets on priority and giving a fresh finishing touch to unsold inventories or wherever required.
In addition to this, the authority claimed to pursue the local bodies to ensure the cleanliness of these areas is not compromised.

Officials claimed that at least 1,650 flats out of nearly 9,000 flats offered in phase I of Sasta Ghar Housing Scheme and Madhyam Vargiya Housing Scheme 2024 are being sold on a first-come, first-served basis at Jasola, Narela, Rohini, Loknayakpuram, Ramgarh, and Siraspur in various categories. Further, out of 169 flats offered through e-auction in Dwarka Housing Scheme in Sector 14, 16B, and 19B of Dwarka under Penthouse, HIG, Super HIG, and MIG categories, over 130 flats have been sold.
